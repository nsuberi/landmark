Draw an area on the map or upload a shapefile to display a list of indigenous and community lands in your area of interest. Then click on your area to view the results.

  • Select a zip file(.zip) containing a shapefile(.shp,.dbf,.prj) from your local file system.
  • The shapefile must be in Geographic Coordinate System (WGS84).
  • The shapefile must be of POLYGON geometry type.
  • The shapefile must not exceed 1 Megabyte.

Indigenous lands are the collectively-held or governed lands and the associated natural resources used by indigenous peoples.

Community lands are the collectively-held or governed lands and the associated natural resources used by communities.

This tool allows you to upload or draw a custom area to analyze indigenous and community lands.
